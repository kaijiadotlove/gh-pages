七夕，星空下的约定

高山流水

繁星闪烁，回忆起古老传说。
牛郎织女，谱写着永恒诗歌。
知己难寻，相遇在偶然时刻。
浩瀚银河，见证那永恒你我。

琴心剑胆

琴声悠扬，星空下演奏乐曲。
余音绕梁，旋律中最美是你。
红袖添香，故事里我愿羁旅。
荡气回肠，天地间永为伴侣。

星空之约

千山万水，怎抵挡衷心情意。
时空相隔，难动摇我心已许。
佳节七夕，赤子心跨越万里。
美梦成真，终相会牛郎织女！

A Promise Under the Starry Sky

高山流水

Stars above twinkle and glow, recalling tales from long ago.
NiuLang and ZhiNü, their eternal song we know.
Souls mates are rare, found in a moment's spree,
The vast Milky Way, witnesses our eternity.

琴心剑胆

Piano notes rise high, under stars they serenely fly.
Echoes dance around, in the melody, you're the most profound.
Red sleeves scent the tale, with you, I'd journey without fail.
Stirring deep emotion, through time and space, an endless devotion.

星空之约

Through mountains vast and rivers wide, true feelings can't be denied.
Time and space may part, but vows of my heart never depart.
On this Qixi fest, with an earnest heart, I travel the quest.
Dreams do come through, as NiuLang and ZhiNü finally rendezvous!
